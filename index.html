Our Story

Born From a Love of Books, Built for Book Lovers

In 2012, with nothing but a small shop, 2,000 books, and an unshakeable belief that the right book changes lives — PageTurner was born. Today, we're Delhi's most trusted literary destination, serving over 150,000 readers.

  • Handpicked selections across 80+ genres — no filler, only books worth your time
  • Expert staff who are readers first — get recommendations that actually match your taste
  • Monthly author events, book clubs & reading festivals open to all members
  • Same-day delivery across Delhi & NCR — your next great read, today
